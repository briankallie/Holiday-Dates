Holidays for all!

Have ordinary days got you down? Ever find yourself looking forward to the next holiday, but not knowing exactly what day it lands on in the calendar year? Or maybe your busy life has caused you to get behind in your Christmas/Valentines/Halloween/etc. shopping? Well, look no further than Holiday Dates! Our site will give you the correct dates for each holiday as well as helpful information about past celebrations, history, and traditions held even in the modern world. Stay a while and browse our holiday list. Who knows? You might find a new holiday to celebrate in the coming years.

Our history

Ever wonder where the idea of Holiday Dates originated? Ever wanted to see the early sketches of the site's design? See our About Us page and explore the history and concept of the site as well as what the site almost became.

Contributions/Corrections

Not finding the holiday you are looking for? Or perhaps the information on an existing entry is lacking/incorrect? Feel free to contribute and add to our listings. We are always looking to expand our collection of holidays and other celebrations. Whether they be religious, regional, secular, or even local; Holiday Dates has you covered!
